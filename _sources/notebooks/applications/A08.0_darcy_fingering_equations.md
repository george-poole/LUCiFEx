# Darcy fingering equations

## Non-dimensionalization

The scales chosen for this problem are the domain thickness $\mathcal{L}=\mathcal{L}_\Omega$, injection velocity $\mathcal{U}=|\textbf{u}_{\text{in}}|$ and $\mathcal{T}=\phi_{\text{ref}}\mathcal{L}/\mathcal{U}$. The non-dimensionalization also shifts to a frame of reference moving at the injection velocity so the non-dimensional velocity and position are

$$
\begin{align*}
\hat{\textbf{u}}&=\frac{\textbf{u}-\textbf{u}_{\text{in}}}{|\textbf{u}_{\text{in}}|} \\
\hat{\textbf{x}}&=\frac{\textbf{x}-t\,\textbf{u}_{\text{in}}/\phi_{\text{ref}}}{\mathcal{L}_\Omega} = \frac{\textbf{x}}{\mathcal{L}_\Omega} - \hat{t}
\end{align*}
$$

The injection Peclet number is

$$Pi = \frac{|\textbf{u}_{\text{in}}|\mathcal{L}_\Omega}{\phi_{\text{ref}}D_{\text{ref}}}$$


## Non-dimensional equations
 
strong form of the initial boundary value problem
$$
\begin{align*}
&\text{Find} \\
&\text{$c(\textbf{x}, t): \Omega\times[0, \infty) \to \mathbb{R}, \textbf{u}(\textbf{x}, t): \Omega\times[0, \infty) \to \mathbb{R}^d$ and $p(\textbf{x}, t): \Omega\times[0, \infty) \to \mathbb{R}$} \\
&\text{such that} \\
&\begin{cases}
\frac{\partial c}{\partial t} + \textbf{u}\cdot\nabla c = \frac{1}{Pi}\nabla\cdot(\mathsf{D}(\phi, \textbf{u})\cdot\nabla c) & \\
\nabla\cdot\textbf{u} = 0 & \\
\textbf{u}=-\frac{\mathsf{K}(\phi)}{\mu(c)}\cdot(\nabla p + \mu\mathsf{K}^{-1}\cdot\textbf{e}_U) & \forall(\textbf{x}, t)\in\Omega\times[0,\infty) \\
c(\textbf{x},t=0)=c_0 & \forall\textbf{x}\in\Omega \\
c=c_{\text{D}} & \forall(\textbf{x}, t)\in\partial\Omega_{\text{D}, c} \times [0,\infty] \\
\textbf{n}\cdot(\mathsf{D}\cdot\nabla c) = c_{\text{N}} & \forall(\textbf{x}, t)\in\partial\Omega_{\text{N}, c}
\times [0,\infty]~,~\partial\Omega_{\text{N}, c}=\partial\Omega/\partial\Omega_{\text{D}, c} \\
\textbf{n}\cdot\textbf{u} = u_{\text{E}} & \forall(\textbf{x}, t)\in\partial\Omega_{\text{E}} \times [0,\infty] \\
p = p_{\text{N}} & \forall(\textbf{x}, t)\in\partial\Omega_{\text{N}}\times [0,\infty]~,~\partial\Omega_{\text{N}}=\partial\Omega/\partial\Omega_{\text{E}}
\end{cases}~.
\end{align*}
$$
