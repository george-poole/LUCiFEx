# Porosity evolution by reaction with a Darcy fluid

initial-boundary value problem on $(\textbf{x}, t)\in\Omega\times[0,\infty)$

$$\phi\frac{\partial c}{\partial t} + \textbf{u}\cdot\nabla c = \nabla\cdot(\mathsf{D}_c\cdot\nabla c) + Ki\,R$$

$$\phi\frac{\partial b}{\partial t} + \textbf{u}\cdot\nabla b = \frac{1}{Le}\nabla\cdot(\mathsf{D}_b\cdot\nabla z)$$

$$\nabla\cdot\textbf{u}=0$$

$$\textbf{u}=-\frac{\mathsf{K}}{\mu}\cdot(\nabla p + Bu\rho\,\textbf{e}_g)$$

$$\varphi\frac{\partial s}{\partial t} = -\varepsilon Ki\, R$$

constitutive relations

$$
\begin{align*}
\mathsf{D}_c &= \mathsf{D}_c(\phi, \textbf{u}) \\ 
\mathsf{D}_c &= \mathsf{D}_b(\phi, \textbf{u}) \\ 
\mathsf{K} &= \mathsf{K}(\phi) \\ 
\mu &= \mu(c,z) \\
\rho &= \rho(c,z) \\
R &= R(s, c)
\end{align*}
$$

initial conditions

$$c(\textbf{x},t=0)=c_0(\textbf{x})$$
$$b(\textbf{x},t=0)=b_0(\textbf{x})$$
$$s(\textbf{x},t=0)=s_0(\textbf{x})$$
