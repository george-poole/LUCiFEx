Porosity evolution-dissolution equations#

Governing equations for solutal convection coupled to Darcy flow and porosity evolution by a \(\text{C}_{(\text{s})}\to \text{C}_{(\text{aq})}\) dissolution reaction.

Dimensional equations#

Strong form#

\[\begin{split} \begin{align*} &\text{Find} \\ &a(\textbf{x}, t): \Omega\times[0, \infty) \to \mathbb{R}, \\ &b(\textbf{x}, t): \Omega\times[0, \infty) \to \mathbb{R}, \\ &c(\textbf{x}, t): \Omega\times[0, \infty) \to \mathbb{R}, \\ &\textbf{u}(\textbf{x}, t): \Omega\times[0, \infty) \to \mathbb{R}^d, \\ &p(\textbf{x}, t): \Omega\times[0, \infty) \to \mathbb{R} \\ &\text{such that} \\ &\begin{cases} \phi\frac{\partial b}{\partial t} + \textbf{u}\cdot\nabla b = \nabla\cdot(\mathsf{D}_b(\phi, \textbf{u})\cdot \nabla c) \\ \phi\frac{\partial c}{\partial t} + \textbf{u}\cdot\nabla c = \nabla\cdot(\mathsf{D}_c(\phi, \textbf{u})\cdot \nabla c) + R \\ \nabla\cdot\textbf{u}=0 \\ \textbf{u}=-\frac{\mathsf{K}}{\mu}\cdot\left(\nabla p + \rho g\,\textbf{e}_g\right) \\ \frac{\partial\phi}{\partial t} = \varepsilon R & \forall(\textbf{x}, t)\in\Omega\times[0,\infty)\\ w=w_0 & \forall(\textbf{x}, t)\in\partial\Omega_{\text{D},w} \times [0,\infty]\quad\forall w\in\{b, c, \phi\}\\ w = w_{\text{D},w} & \forall(\textbf{x}, t)\in\partial\Omega_{\text{D},w} {0}\quad\forall w\in\{b, c\}\\ \textbf{n}\cdot(\mathsf{D}_w\nabla w) = w_{\text{N}, w} & \forall(\textbf{x}, t)\in\partial\Omega_{\text{N},w} \times [0,\infty]\quad\forall w\in\{b, c\}\\ \textbf{n}\cdot\textbf{u} = u_{\text{E}} & \forall(\textbf{x}, t)\in\partial\Omega_{\text{E}} \times [0,\infty] \\ p = p_{\text{N}} & \forall(\textbf{x}, t)\in\partial\Omega_{\text{N}}\times [0,\infty]~,~\partial\Omega_{\text{N}}=\partial\Omega/\partial\Omega_{\text{E}} \end{cases}~. \end{align*} \end{split}\]

Specification#

\[\begin{split} \mathbb{S}= \begin{cases} \phi(\textbf{x}) \\ \mathsf{K}(\phi) \\ \mathsf{D}_b(\phi, \textbf{u}) \\ \mathsf{D}_c(\phi, \textbf{u}) \\ \rho(b,c) \\ \mu(b,c) \\ R(b, c, \phi) \end{cases} \end{split}\]
