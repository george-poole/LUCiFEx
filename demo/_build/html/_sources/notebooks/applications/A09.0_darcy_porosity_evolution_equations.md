# Darcy porosity evolution-dissolution equations

Governing equations for solutal convection coupled to Darcy flow and porosity evolution by a $\text{C}_{(\text{s})}\to \text{C}_{(\text{aq})}$ dissolution reaction.

## Dimensional equations

### Strong form

$$
\begin{align*}
&\text{Find} \\
&\phi(\textbf{x}, t): \Omega\times[0, \infty) \to \mathbb{R},  \\
&b(\textbf{x}, t): \Omega\times[0, \infty) \to \mathbb{R},  \\
&c(\textbf{x}, t): \Omega\times[0, \infty) \to \mathbb{R},  \\
&\textbf{u}(\textbf{x}, t): \Omega\times[0, \infty) \to \mathbb{R}^d, \\
&p(\textbf{x}, t): \Omega\times[0, \infty) \to \mathbb{R} \\
&\text{such that} \\
&\begin{cases}
\frac{\partial\phi}{\partial t} = \varepsilon R \\
\phi\frac{\partial b}{\partial t} + \textbf{u}\cdot\nabla b = \nabla\cdot(\mathsf{D}_b(\phi, \textbf{u})\cdot \nabla c) \\
\phi\frac{\partial c}{\partial t} + \textbf{u}\cdot\nabla c = \nabla\cdot(\mathsf{D}_c(\phi, \textbf{u})\cdot \nabla c) + R \\
\nabla\cdot\textbf{u}=0 \\
\textbf{u}=-\frac{\mathsf{K}}{\mu}\cdot\left(\nabla p + \rho g\,\textbf{e}_g\right) & \forall(\textbf{x}, t)\in\Omega\times[0,\infty)\\
w=w_0  & \forall(\textbf{x}, t)\in\Omega\times\{0\}\quad\forall w\in\{b, c, \phi\}\\
w = w_{\text{D},w} & \forall(\textbf{x}, t)\in\partial\Omega_{\text{D},w} {0}\quad\forall w\in\{b, c\}\\
\textbf{n}\cdot(\mathsf{D}_w\nabla w) = w_{\text{N}, w} & \forall(\textbf{x}, t)\in\partial\Omega_{\text{N},w} \times [0,\infty]\quad\forall w\in\{b, c\}\\
\textbf{n}\cdot\textbf{u} = u_{\text{E}} & \forall(\textbf{x}, t)\in\partial\Omega_{\text{E}} \times [0,\infty] \\
p = p_{\text{N}} & \forall(\textbf{x}, t)\in\partial\Omega_{\text{N}}\times [0,\infty]~,~\partial\Omega_{\text{N}}=\partial\Omega/\partial\Omega_{\text{E}}
\end{cases} \\
&\text{given} \\
&\mathbb{S}=
\begin{cases}
\Omega\subset\mathbb{R}^d & \text{domain} \\
w_0~\forall w\in\{\phi, b, c\} & \text{initial conditions} \\
\mathsf{K}(\phi) & \text{permeability} \\
\mathsf{D}_w(\phi, \textbf{u})~\forall w\in\{b,c\} & \text{solutal dispersions}\\
\rho(b,c) \\
\mu(b,c) \\
R(b, c, \phi)
\end{cases}
\end{align*}
$$
