# Governing equations for the convection of a Darcy-Brinkman fluid in partially porous medium

## Dimensional equations

Working in the Boussinesq approximation and with uniform viscosity.

initial-boundary value problem on $(\textbf{x}, t)\in\Omega\times[0,\infty)$

$$\phi\frac{\partial c}{\partial t} + \textbf{u}\cdot\nabla c=\nabla\cdot(\mathsf{D}\cdot\nabla c)$$

$$\nabla\cdot\textbf{u}=0$$

$$\rho_{\text{ref}}\left(\frac{\partial\textbf{u}}{\partial t}+\textbf{u}\cdot\nabla(\textbf{u}/\phi)\right)=-\phi\nabla p + \mu_{\text{ref}}\nabla^2\textbf{u}+\phi\rho g\,\textbf{e}_g - \mu_{\text{ref}}\,\phi\mathsf{K}^{-1}\cdot\textbf{u}$$

constitutive relations
$$
\begin{align*}
\phi &= \phi(\textbf{x}) \\
\mathsf{D} &= \mathsf{D}(\phi, \textbf{u}) \\ 
\mathsf{K} &= \mathsf{K}(\phi) \\ 
\rho &= \rho(c)
\end{align*}
$$

initial conditions

$$c(\textbf{x},t=0)=c_0(\textbf{x})$$

$$\textbf{u}(\textbf{x},t=0)=\textbf{u}_0(\textbf{x})$$

$$p(\textbf{x},t=0)=p_0(\textbf{x})$$

Dirichlet boundary condition on $\textbf{x}\in\partial\Omega_{\text{D}}$

$$c = c_{\text{D}}$$

Neumann boundary condition on $\textbf{x}\in\partial\Omega_{\text{N}}=\partial\Omega/\partial\Omega_{\text{D}}$

$$\textbf{n}\cdot\nabla c = c_{\text{N}}$$

essential boundary condition on $\textbf{x}\in\partial\Omega_E$

$$\textbf{u}=\textbf{u}_{\text{E}}$$

natural boundary condition on $\textbf{x}\in\partial\Omega_{\text{N}}=\partial\Omega/\partial\Omega_{\text{E}}$

$$\sigma\cdot\textbf{n} = \boldsymbol{\sigma}_{\text{N}}$$

## Non-dimensionalization


### Scalings

length scale $|\textbf{x}|\sim\mathcal{L}$

velocity scale $|\textbf{u}|\sim\mathcal{U}$

time scale $t\sim\mathcal{T}$

concentration scale $c\sim\Delta c$

buoyancy scale $\rho g\sim g \Delta\rho$

permeability scale $K\sim K_{\text{ref}}$

diffusivity scale $|\mathsf{D}|\sim D_{\text{ref}}$

pressure scale $p\sim\rho_{\text{ref}}\,\mathcal{U}\mathcal{L}/\mathcal{T}$

domain length scale $\mathcal{L}_\Omega$

### Dimensionless numbers

Prandtl number 
$$Pr=\frac{\mu_{\text{ref}}}{\rho_{\text{ref}}D_{\text{ref}}}$$

Rayleigh number

$$Ra=\frac{\mathcal{L}^3_\Omega g\Delta\rho}{\mu_{\text{ref}}D_{\text{ref}}}$$

Darcy number

$$Da = \frac{K_{\text{ref}}}{\mathcal{L}_\Omega^2}$$

dimensionless domain length 
$$Xl=\frac{\mathcal{L}_\Omega}{\mathcal{L}}$$

### Non-dimensional equations

$$\phi\frac{\partial c}{\partial t}+Ad\,\textbf{u}\cdot\nabla c=\frac{1}{Pe}\nabla\cdot(\mathsf{D}\cdot\nabla c) $$

$$\nabla\cdot\textbf{u}=0$$

$$\frac{\partial\textbf{u}}{\partial t}+Ad\,\textbf{u}\cdot\nabla(\textbf{u}/\phi)=-\phi\nabla p + Vi\,\nabla^2\textbf{u} + Bu\,\phi\rho\,\textbf{e}_g - Pm\,\phi\mathsf{K}^{-1}\cdot\textbf{u}$$

$$Ad=\frac{\mathcal{U}\mathcal{T}}{\mathcal{L}}$$

$$Pe=\frac{\mathcal{L}^2}{D_{\text{ref}}\mathcal{T}}$$

$$Vi=\frac{\mu_{\text{ref}}\mathcal{T}}{\rho_{\text{ref}}\mathcal{L}^2}$$

$$Bu=\frac{\,g\Delta\rho\,\mathcal{T}}{\rho_{\text{ref}}\,\mathcal{U}}$$

$$Pm=\frac{\mu_{\text{ref}}\,\,\mathcal{T}}{K_{\text{ref}}\,\rho_{\text{ref}}\,\mathcal{U}}$$

### Mapping to non-dimensionalized equations for the convection of a Navier-Stokes fluid ($\phi=1$)

$$
\begin{Bmatrix}
Ad \\
Pe \\
Vi\,\sigma \\
Bu\,\rho\,\textbf{e}_g
\end{Bmatrix}
\mapsto
\begin{Bmatrix}
Ad \\
Pe \\
-p\mathsf{I} + \tfrac{1}{2}Vi(\nabla\textbf{u} + \nabla\textbf{u}^{\mathsf{T}})\\ 
Bu\,\rho\,\textbf{e}_g - Pm\,\mathsf{K}^{-1}\cdot\textbf{u}
\end{Bmatrix}
$$
